Emergency Preparedness Bag

The Earthquake Bag

Earthquakes can cause great destruction. We suggest the Gregory Stout 35 Backpack.

  • Item List
  • Emergency plan with extra phone numbers.
  • Medication.
  • Food and water for a minimum of 72 hours (for you and your family).
  • A water bottle with a filter.
  • Can or a kettle to heat up food and water.
  • Flashlight.
  • Toiletries.
  • Fire starter (lighter, paper, paste).
  • Change of clothes.
  • Copies of all your important information (birth certificate, identification, house/insurance papers and more).
  • First Aid kit.
  • Charger for your phone and smart devices (solar option).
  • Extra cash.
  • Pen and paper.
  • Dust masks.
  • A radio (crank option)
  • Sleeping bag.
  • Knife or a multitool.
  • Duct tape, rope and a tarp.
