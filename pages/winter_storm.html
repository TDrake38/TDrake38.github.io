Emergency Preparedness Bag

The Winter Storm Bag

During winter months your bag may have to be larger to include winter gear. We suggest the 75 litre Gregory Denali.

  • Item List
  • Emergency plan.
  • Food and water for a minimum of 72 hours (for you and your family).
  • Toiletries.
  • Flashlight with extra batteries.
  • Fire starter (lighter, paper, paste).
  • Emergency blanket.
  • Flares.
  • Change of clothes (especially extra socks).
  • Copies of all your important information (birth certificate, identification, house/insurance papers and more).
  • First Aid kit.
  • Charger for your phone and smart devices (solar option).
  • Extra cash.
  • Flagging ribbon and a marker.
  • Pen and paper.
  • A sleeping bag for sub zero degrees.
  • Toque/winter hat.
  • Hot paws for hands and feet.
  • Warm Jacket.
  • Winter gloves.
  • Snowshoes and ski poles.
  • Slip on ice cleats.
  • Polar sunglasses to help with snow blinding.
  • Axe.
  • Small foldable shovel.
