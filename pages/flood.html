Emergency Preparedness Bag

The Flood Bag

With a flood or a tsunami usually comes a lot of water. In this case you're going to want a little bit bigger of a bag to hold a few more articles of clothing. We recommend the Black Diamond Mission 55 Backpack from MEC. A 55L bag. With the extra space there is lots of room to keep things dry. Click the bag.

  • Item List
  • Emergency plan.
  • Two days worth of food and water.
  • Toiletries.
  • Flash light.
  • Fishing hook and line. With some kind of artificial bait.
  • Fire starter (lighter, paper, paste).
  • Change of clothes.
  • Copies of all your important information (birth certificate, identification, house/insurance papers and more).
  • First Aid kit.
  • Charger for your phone and smart devices (solar option).
  • Extra cash.
  • Pen and paper.
  • Waterproof pouch for your important documents.
  • Flare(s).
  • Whistle.
  • Waterproof matches.
  • Knife or can opener.
  • Duck tape, rope and plastic to make a shelter. Or a small tent.
  • Air mattress (can help with floating).
