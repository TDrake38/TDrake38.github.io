Emergency Preparedness Bag

The Hurricane Bag

Hurricanes come with substantial wind and heavy rain.
The Osprey Kestrel is a great starting bag. Click the bag icon.

  • Item List
  • Emergency plan.
  • Medication.
  • Food and water for a minimum of 72 hours (for you and your family).
  • Mirror for signaling help.
  • Toiletries.
  • Flashlight.
  • Fire starter (lighter, paper, paste).
  • Change of clothes (especially extra socks).
  • Copies of all your important information (birth certificate, identification, house/insurance papers and more).
  • Waterproof pouch for your important documents.
  • First Aid kit.
  • Bug spray.
  • Charger for your phone and smart devices (solar option).
  • Extra cash.
  • Pen and paper.
  • Knife and a can opener.
  • Tarp and tape.
  • A radio (crank option).
  • Photo of your loved ones.
  • Waterproof matches.
  • Fishing hook and line with some kind of artificial bait.
  • Can or a kettle to heat up food and water.
