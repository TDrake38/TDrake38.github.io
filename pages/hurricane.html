Emergency Preparedness Bag

The Hurricane Bag

Hurricanes are not fun. They come with a lot of wind and water. You may need items to make a shelter if it makes a mess of your home. A solid sized bag to go with is a 38 litre bag. The Osprey Kestrel from MEC is a great starting bag for this. Click the bag.

  • Item List
  • Emergency plan.
  • Medication.
  • Two days worth of food and water.
  • Mirror for signalling help.
  • Toiletries.
  • Flash light.
  • Fire starter (lighter, paper, paste).
  • Change of clothes.
  • Copies of all your important information (birth certificate, identification, house/insurance papers and more).
  • First Aid kit.
  • Bug spray.
  • Charger for your phone and smart devices (solar option).
  • Extra cash.
  • Pen and paper.
  • Knife and a can opener.
  • Plastic and tape to make a shelter.
  • A radio. One with a crank because you may not be able to get batteries very often.
  • Photo of your loved ones.
  • Waterproof matches.
  • Waterproof pouch for your important documents.
  • Fishing hook and line. With some kind of artificial bait.
  • Extra socks.
  • Can or a kettle to heat up food and water.
